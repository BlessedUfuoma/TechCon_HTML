Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for software developers and has since grown into a global conference drawing thousands of participants annually. Over the years, it has become a platform for emerging technologies and bold ideas.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower innovators, creators, and thought leaders to drive the future of technology through education, collaboration, and inspiration. We believe in open knowledge, diversity, and sustainable progress.
